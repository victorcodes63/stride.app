# Bi-weekly payslips — days worked

## Working week

- **Monday–Saturday** count as working days (Sunday off).
- **Period 1** = calendar days **1–15** of the payroll month.
- **Period 2** = **16th → last day** of that month.

## Selecting attendance

1. Open **Payroll** → edit the employee row (bi-weekly client).
2. Under **Days worked (payslip)**, tick each day the employee **attended**.
3. **Save** — attendance is stored on the payroll row and appears on **print payslips** and **email PDF/HTML** (when sent).

## Pro-rating period grosses (optional)

If pay should follow days worked:

1. Tick only the days they worked.
2. Click **Pro-rate period grosses from days…**

Formula per period:

`(monthly basic ÷ 2) × (days ticked ÷ working days in that period)`

Then statutory (PAYE, NSSF, SHIF, AHL) is recalculated on the **new** combined period grosses.

Allowances are **not** auto pro-rated; add manually if needed.

## Payslip text

Each period shows:

- Gross for that period  
- **X days** worked  
- Short list of dates (e.g. Mon 3, Tue 4, …)
